Intro

Hi, I am Anuradha Naik

I am a software engineer and a digital growth professional. I have spent my days developing applications which benefit the community and I advocate for the dignity and autonomy of all people. My motivation is to leave a better world for those coming after us and I have been blessed with the opportunity to work with vendors around the globe, help manage platforms serving an audience of 10M+ and whole lot more & I look forward to doing much more!
I have expanded and automated technical operations at media companies, non profit operations & POC owned businesses. I have also been a part of content creation, content strategy and messaging for presitigious non-profit organizations. I believe tech can benefit everyone. The creativity and ingenuity I see in information technology and the lives it has changed has blown me away and I am doing my best to contribute to it. Everyday for me is about growing and coding for the greater good!

Campus Safety

Advocacy

I spent 4 years advocating and helping move forward the New York Health Act (Guaranteed heathcare for each resident New Yorker). Coming from a family of Gynaecs, I know what it takes to bring someone ino this earth. Motivated by the ongoing issues around healthcare everyone in the US faced thanks to the healthcare industry, I did everything I possibly could to help get this done. Key Highlights were to help get our issue trending on twitter and see the reintroduction this bill with majority cosponsorship in both houses of state government in 2021. I learnt a LOT from the very best thanks to this bill, It took all the grit I never knew I had to pursue this.

    My Contributions
  • Regular / Daily Video EOD updates about the New York Health Act
  • Canvassing / Phonebanking / Lobby Visits
  • Helping run social media campaigns on twitter and facebook
  • Create Posts, Graphics and videos for the campaign and campaign partners

As mentioned in previous sections, I joined 350nyc to learn and advocate for the environment.
